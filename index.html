Role of Technology in Learning

Introduction

Technology has become a very important part of education. It helps students learn faster, easier, and in a more interesting way. Today’s education is a smart blend of books and digital tools.
People call this era of digital learning.

Access to Information

Students can search the WWW to gain knowledge from any corner of the world. Videos, articles, and apps provide information anytime.

Interactive Learning

Technology makes learning fun. For example, mathematical formulas like E = mc² become easier through animations. In chemistry, we also use H₂O to explain water with the help of diagrams.
